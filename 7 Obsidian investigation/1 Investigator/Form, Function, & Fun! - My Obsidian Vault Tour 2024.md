The [[Zettelkasten]] organizing system is divided into three categories_

**fleeting:** <u>thoughts and ideas that you capture throughout your day</u>. this are meant to be <u>in depth</u> and serves as a jumping off point for future thinking and review
**literature:** notes or <u>resources that you didn’t create yourself.</u> these can be articles, books, quotes, videos or anything along those lines
**permanent:** <u>mix of both</u>. more refined combination of both fleeting and literature notes. the key to taking advantages of these combinations is <u>linking: the practice of referencing your notes across each other. </u>obsidian is designed around this practice and helps you visualize the links you create as your Vault grows now all that's great for keeping track of thoughts studying different subjects and building knowledge in a constructive and organized way.
